Banana Smoothie

Brief Description

This is the smoothie I make before the gym or when I need a solid drink for breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 300ml-400ml of Almond Mink
  • 1 large ripe banana
  • 1 spoon Peanut butter of choice (I use the ones with chunks)
  • 1 spoon honey
  • 1 scoop of 100% Real Whey Protein (Banana/Vanilla flavour)
  • 30g chocolate Granola
  • Cinnamon to taste

Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients except for the granola in a blender
  2. Serve in your cup of choice
  3. Sprinkle the granola on top
  4. Enjoy your nutritious drink
